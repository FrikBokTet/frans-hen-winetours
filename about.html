About Us

Inspired by Heritage. Built on Connection.

Frans & Hen was inspired on 04 April 2025.

This idea originated at a Family outing at the well-known place “Franschhoek Wine Trams”. This inspired us to start with this project and work collectively towards a holistic future to embrace a foundation where everyone is part of our Intimate, Authentic, and Unforgettable journey.

From that day forward, Frans & Hen became more than just a name, it became a collaborative journey, driven by passion and purpose. We committed ourselves to crafting an experience that isn't just a product or service, but a foundation, one where everyone feels seen, included, and valued.
