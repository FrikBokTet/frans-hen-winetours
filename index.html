Our Story

Frans & Hen Wine Tours Pty Ltd is a premier luxury wine tour operator based in Caledon, Western Cape South Africa. We specialize in exclusive, immersive wine tourism experiences across the renowned Hemel-en-Aarde Valley and Botrivier Wine Routes. Our service caters to high-value visitors, offering curated tours that include luxury transport, professional tour guides, and authentic cultural insights. A space where the intimate, the authentic, and the unforgettable are not just ideals, but lived realities.

Hemel & Aarde

This world-renowned wine valley, whose name means "Heaven and Earth", lives up to every bit of its name. Explore over 15 exceptional wine farms, including Creation Wines, ranked among the Top 3 world-class wineries globally. Whether you're a wine enthusiast, a family on holiday, or a group of friends seeking a shared adventure, there's something for everyone — from vineyard tastings to picturesque moments to fine food pairings. This is more than a wine tour — it's a slice of heaven on earth.

Botriver

This isn't just a wine tour — it's a memory tour. With over 10 unique wine experiences to choose from, the Botrivier region invites you to slow down and savor life with loved ones. Rich in charm and character, each estate tells its own story, offering an intimate and heartwarming day for families, friends, and wine lovers alike. It's the kind of day you'll want to relive again and again.
