Our Story

Frans & Hen Wine Tours Pty Ltd is a premier luxury wine tour operator based in Caledon, Western Cape South Africa. We specialize in exclusive, immersive wine tourism experiences across the renowned Hemel-en-Aarde Valley and Botrivier Wine Routes. Our service caters to high-value visitors, offering curated tours that include luxury transport, professional tour guides, and authentic cultural insights. A space where the intimate, the authentic, and the unforgettable are not just ideals, but lived realities.
